Akira Momose First Love Is A Failure


Author: Harekawa Shinta


Genre: Manga, Romance, Comedy, Slice of Life, Harem, School Life

Kugayama Hajime, a boy living in the countryside, comes back to his old classmate Momose Akira, whom he used to admire. Just as he is about to watch her from afar and enjoy his sweet and sour youth, Akira makes an unexpected approach. And there was a "fate" that was more than he could have imagined.
